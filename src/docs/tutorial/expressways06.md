For exits connecting to your busiest Boulevards, use a tight little roundabout like this. Hit that ![](IconGrid)Gridless Mode [TAB] to make everything close together.
![](docs/images/tutorial/expressways/expressways-6.png)

If you can get it so that there's only two roads leading _in_ to the intersection (not counting roads that only lead out of the intersection), the safety folks will let us remove the stop light. This is called "The Two Input Rule". Free flowing driving on and off the highway -- Radical!
